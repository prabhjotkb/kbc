Well come to the "Who wants to be a millioniere"

The rules of the game is simple . There will be 15 questions one by one which you have to answer them. you can give answers by clicking on options or by voice commands . As you give right answers , you will won money as shown in the right hand side of the game. For your assist there are two life-lifes available .



First is 50-50 . by using this life-line , two wrong options will be vanished . use this life-line if you have confussion between two options.

Another life is audience's pole . here audience will be answering your question . by using this life line , there will be a chart which shows you the probiblity of a option to be right answer.

Voice commands for answering .

  • For A = A final answer
  • For B = B final answer
  • For C = C final answer
  • For D = D final answer
